About CROSS

CROSS stands for Christ Reigns Over Students' Soul. Our mission is to support and guide students and individuals through their spiritual journey every day with uplifting content, scripture, and connection to community.

Designed with love and purpose, the CROSS app helps you reflect, discover new insights, and never lose touch with your faith.
